Guidlines

Total number of squad joining the match will be 20 only. First of all each team will play 5 TDM matches. 4 teams will be selected for Playoff having the highest points. 1st, 2nd, 3rd and 4th positions will be given in accordance with their points. In the Playoff round the teams with 1st and 2nd rank will play a match (Qualifier 1). The winner will be the first finalist and the losing team will play Qualifier 2. Again the teams with 3rd and 4th position will play a match (Eliminator). The winner will go to the Qualifier 2 and the loosing team will be disqualified. In Qualifier 2 the winner will be the 2nd finalist and the losing team will be disqualified. And lastly the winner of the final match (1st), runner up of the final match (2nd) and runner up of Qualifier 2 (3rd) will be rewarded.
